About this service

Role: Conference Alumni (CA)

Time: 2018, 2019, 2020 (virtual session), 2023

Impact: As a Conference Alumni, I helped create an energetic, welcoming environment that encouraged Young Leaders (YLs) to take risks, reflect, and grow. CAs connect with YLs one-on-one, facilitate group discussion, and promote inclusion — contributing directly to memorable and transformative conference experiences.

Primary responsibilities

  • Serve as a peer mentor and primary connection for a small homegroup of Young Leaders throughout the weekend.
  • Lead conversations, encourage participation, and support personal growth and confidence-building activities.
  • Attend required training sessions to learn effective facilitation techniques and safety protocols.
  • Stay on-site for the full conference weekend to support logistics and participant needs.

What I learned / contributions I made

  • Improved my leadership and communication skills by facilitating small-group discussions and debriefs.
  • Gained experience mentoring younger participants and helping them set goals and reflect on personal growth.
  • Contributed to a safe, inclusive conference culture that enabled peers to try new things and build confidence.
  • Assisted with recruitment efforts and helped promote the conference to local high schools.
