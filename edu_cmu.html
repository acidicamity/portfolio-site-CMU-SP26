Courses I'm Taking This Semester: Fall 2025

  • CPS 382: Advanced Multimedia Design - Design interactive multimedia projects using advanced authoring tools and discuss issues concerning representation, storage, compression, transmission, and manipulation of various multimedia components.
  • ENG 591: Creative Writing Fiction - This course is an advanced course in short fiction writing where students will spend the semester practicing their craft, honing their critical reading skills, and giving and receiving comments in a friendly but rigorous workshop setting.
  • ART 224: 3D Computer Animation - This course is an introduction to the 3D graphic production environment. Students develop basic techniques in 3D modeling, texturing, and animation.
  • JRN 220: Basic Media Photography - Students develop skills of image creation, digital processing of images, interpersonal communication, time management, file organization and occupational ethical, legal and historical investigation within the context of journalism.
  • CPS 165: Modern Website Design - Explores the design and development of dynamic and interactive user-friendly websites. Students learn to maintain a web portfolio and about web publishing best practices with consideration of social media.
